Telemetry

Last updated: November 4, 2022

Spyglass collects usage data (only metadata, never any search terms or results) to measure feature usage, discover product issues, and to help guide what we prioritize. Selling usage data will never be part of our business model, it is used solely to improve the end-user experience.

We use Sentry for crash reporting and MixPanel for telemetry.
